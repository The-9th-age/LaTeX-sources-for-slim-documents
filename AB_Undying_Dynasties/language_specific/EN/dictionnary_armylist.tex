\newcommand{\divinelight}{Divine Light}
\newcommand{\divinelightdef}{%
Enemy Wizards within \distance{36} of one or more Caskets of Phatep suffer a \minuss{}1 modifier to their casting rolls. When a Casket of Phatep is removed as a casualty, all units within \distance{12} suffer 3D3+3 hits with Strength 1 and Armour Penetration 10.%
}
\newcommand{\phatepscurse}{Phatep's Curse}
\newcommand{\phatepscursedef}{%
Unless this model made an Advance Move or March Move during the current Player Turn, it can cast the following Bound Spell with Power Level (6/6):\newline%
Type: \damage{}, \hex{}, \range{36}. Duration: \instant{}.\newline%
The target \textbf{must} take a Discipline Test rolling an additional D6. If failed, the target suffers a number of hits equal to
the amount by which the test was failed. Hits are resolved with Armour Penetration 10 and wound automatically.%
}
