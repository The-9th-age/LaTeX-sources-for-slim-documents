% Tomb Architect

\newcommand{\masterofstone}{Master of Stone}
\newcommand{\masterofstonedef}{%
Right before the battle (during step 7 of the Deployment Phase Sequence), and at the beginning of each friendly Player Turn, choose a friendly unit consisting entirely of models with \ensouledstatue{} within \distance{18} of the Tomb Architect. }

% Casket of Phatep

\newcommand{\divinelight}{Divine Light}
\newcommand{\divinelightdef}{%
Enemy Wizards within \distance{36} of one or more Caskets of Phatep suffer a -1 modifier to their casting rolls. When a Casket of Phatep is removed as a casualty, all units within \distance{12} suffer 3D3+3 hits with Strength 1 and Armour Penetration 10.%
}
\newcommand{\phatepscurse}{Phatep's Curse}
\newcommand{\phatepscursedef}{%
Unless this model made an Advance Move or March Move during the current Player Turn, it can cast the following Bound Spell with Power Level (6/6):\newline%
Type: \damage{}, \hex{}, \range{36}. Duration: \instant{}.\newline%
The target must take a Discipline Test rolling an additional D6. If failed, the target suffers a number of hits equal to
the amount by which the test was failed. Hits are resolved with Armour Penetration 10 and wound automatically.%
}

% Ark of Ages

\newcommand{\sacredark}{Sacred Ark}
\newcommand{\sacredarkdef}{%
All friendly Wizards within \distance{12} of the Ark of Ages add \distance{+6} to the range of their non-Bound Spells. Spells of type \aura{} only add \distance{+3} to their range.\par%
Furthermore, you gain the Well of Souls Flux Card. One use only. May be activated at the start of any of your Magic Phases. Instead of randomly drawing a Flux Card from the deck, you draw the Well of Souls Flux Card. In addition, instead of randomly drawing a Flux Card from the deck, your opponent must draw the Well of Souls Flux Card in the following Magic Phase. Afterwards discard the Well of Souls Flux Card (even if the opponent did not use it for whatever reason).\par%
\wellofsoulslayout{%
		\wellofsoulstitle{Well of Souls}{Flux Card}%
	}{%
		\fluxcard{5 Magic Dice}{both players}{5 Veil Tokens}{Active Player}%
	}{%
		When making a casting roll with 3 or more Magic Dice, all doubles count as triples. Note that a Caster can suffer multiple Miscast effects from a single casting roll. However no Miscast effect can be applied more than once.%
	}%
}

% Name already defined
\newcommand{\charnelcatapultdef}{%
This Artillery Weapon \textbf{always} hits on a roll equal to or greater than its Aim. It can be fired in two ways:%
\begin{smallitemize}%
\item As a \textbf{\catapult{4}} with \range{12-60}, \shots{1}, \St{} 3 [7], \AP{} 0 [4], [\multiplewounds{D3, \clippedwings{}}{}].%
\item As a \textbf{\catapult{6}} with \range{12-48}, \shots{1}, \St{} 3, \AP{} 0, \flamingattacks{}, \magicalattacks{}. Units that suffer at least one casualty from this weapon must take a Panic Test just as if they had suffered \SI{25}{\percent} casualties. Panic Tests caused by this weapon are taken at -1 Discipline.%
\end{smallitemize}%
}
