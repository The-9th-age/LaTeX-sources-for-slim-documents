The bearer gains \textbf{Fortitude (4+)} and \textbf{Multiple Wounds (2, against Large and Infantry)}.

\newcommand{\hoardmasterdef}{%
\itemrestriction{Great Khans and Khans only.}
The bearer gains \textbf{Weapon Master}, Plate Armour, Iron Fist, Great Weapon, Halberd and Paired Weapons. The bearer cannot take Weapon Enchantments.
}

}

\newcommand{\wildheartdef}{%
\itemrestriction{Mammoth Hunters only. \oneofakind{}.}
The bearer loses Not a Leader and must be the General. Its Special Item allowance is increased to 150 pts. Another Mammoth Hunter in the army may be the Battle Standard Bearer for 50 pts; this Battle Standard Bearer gains Scrapling Lookout when joined to Yeti units. The Core limit is reduced to \enquote{Min. \SI{20}{\percent}}. The army may not include any Great Khans, Khans, Bruisers, Mercenary Veterans, Bombardiers, or Thunder Cannons.

}
