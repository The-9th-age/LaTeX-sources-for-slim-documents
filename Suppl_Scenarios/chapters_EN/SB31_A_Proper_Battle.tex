\subsection*{Winning the Scenario}

\rewordedrule{During the game, a player gains one Victory Counter if they are the first to be in any of the following situation:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \rewordedrule{An} enemy \rewordedrule{S}coring \rewordedrule{U}nit \rewordedrule{is removed as a casualty.}
\item \rewordedrule{A friendly unit successfully Charges an enemy unit.}
\item \rewordedrule{A}n enemy unit \rewordedrule{of Gigantic Height or with War Machine is removed as a casualty}.
\end{itemize}

At the end of the game\rewordedrule{, determine each player's most expensive unit that was removed as a casualty (based on the Point Costs on the Army List; in case of Combined Units, count joined Characters separately). T}he player \rewordedrule{with the less expensive unit out of these units} gains 1 \rewordedrule{Victory Counter}\rewordedrule{, and each} player \rewordedrule{gains 1 Victory Counter if their opponent's General has been removed as a casualty}.\newline
\rewordedrule{T}he player with the most \rewordedrule{Victory Counters} wins the game.
