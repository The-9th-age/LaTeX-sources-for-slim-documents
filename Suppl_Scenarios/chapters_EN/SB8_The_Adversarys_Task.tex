\subsection*{Deployment}

Deployment Zones are areas more than \distance{10} away from \rewordedrule{the Centre Line}. Characters cannot be deployed within \distance{18} of the short \rewordedrule{Board} \rewordedrule{E}dges.

\subsection*{Scenario Special Rules}

\rewordedrule{During step 7 of the Deployment Phase Sequence (after moving Vanguarding units and before rolling for first turn),} both players take turns in openly choosing three Characters\rewordedrule{from their opponent's Army List, starting with the player that finished deploying first. If a there are less than three Characters in an Army List, the opponent automatically chooses all Characters instead}. Characters currently \rewordedrule{not} on the \rewordedrule{board} (\rewordedrule{e.g. if Ambushing}) may still be chosen.

\subsection*{Winning the Secondary Objective}

At the end of the game, the player that has the highest number of \rewordedrule{their} chosen Characters still alive wins th\rewordedrule{is} Secondary Objective.
