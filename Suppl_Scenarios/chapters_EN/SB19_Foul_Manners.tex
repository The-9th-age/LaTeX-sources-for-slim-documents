\subsection*{Pre-Game Set-up}

\rewordedrule{During step 7 of the Deployment Phase Sequence (after moving Vanguarding units and before rolling for first turn)}, both players must choose \rewordedrule{one of their own units}, starting with the player that finished deploying first. The chosen units gain \textbf{Foul Manners}.

\subsection*{Scenario Special Rules}

\subsubsection*{Foul Manners}

All units \rewordedrule{gain} \textbf{Frenzy} \rewordedrule{with the exception that only enemy} unit\rewordedrule{s} with Foul Manners \rewordedrule{trigger a Frenzy Test for this instance of Frenzy.}

Each time an enemy unit fails a Frenzy \rewordedrule{Test} \rewordedrule{triggered by a unit with} Foul Manners, you gain a Victory Counter. If the \rewordedrule{enemy} unit \rewordedrule{is} Scoring, you gain an additional Victory Counter. 

\subsection*{Winning the Secondary Objective}

At the end of the game, the player with the most Victory Counters wins th\rewordedrule{is} Secondary Objective.
