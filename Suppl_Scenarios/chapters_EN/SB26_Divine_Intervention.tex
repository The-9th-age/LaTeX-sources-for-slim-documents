\subsection*{Winning the Scenario}

Each time \rewordedrule{an} Incarnate Beast performs any of the following \rewordedrule{actions}, \rewordedrule{its owner} gains a Victory Counter:

\begin{itemize}
\item Successfully \rewordedrule{C}harges \rewordedrule{an enemy unit}
\item Kills an enemy \rewordedrule{C}haracter
\item Wins a Round of Combat
\end{itemize}

Each time \rewordedrule{an} Incarnate Beast \rewordedrule{loses a Health Point}, the opponent \rewordedrule{of its owner} gains a Victory Counter.

At the end of the game, the player with the most Victory Counters wins the game.
