
\newgamingscenario{2}{4}{The Hidden Kings}
\label{TheHiddenKings}

\flufffont{Two scheming lords vie for the throne and the time has come for a decisive battle. However, they will both need to be in the thick of it at the point of victory to claim absolute authority in the moment of victory.}

\subsection*{Deployment}

Deployment Zones are areas more than \distance{10} away from th\rewordedrule{e Centre Line}. Only models that are Cavalry, have Light Troops and/or Scout may be deployed within \distance{12} of the short \rewordedrule{Board E}dges.

When declaring Special Deployment, players may choose to keep up to two of their units as reinforcement. These units follow the rules for Ambush\rewordedrule{ing units}, except \rewordedrule{that} they automatically arrive in Game Turn 1\rewordedrule{,} and must be placed touching the \rewordedrule{owner's} long \rewordedrule{Board E}dge.

\subsection*{Pre-Game Set-up}

\rewordedrule{Immediately b}efore \rewordedrule{determining the Deployment Zones}, both players must secretly choose one of the following:

\begin{itemize}
\item Any \rewordedrule{friendly} non-\rewordedrule{C}haracter unit to conceal the army's Hidden King
\item Any \rewordedrule{friendly} Character to be the army's Hidden King
\end{itemize}

Write the \rewordedrule{chosen} unit/\rewordedrule{C}haracter down on a piece of paper.

\subsection*{Scenario Special Rules}

At the start of each \rewordedrule{P}layer \rewordedrule{T}urn, the \rewordedrule{A}ctive \rewordedrule{P}layer may \rewordedrule{choose one of the Reactive Player's units. This player must reveal if the Hidden King resides in this unit. He doesn't have to reveal who the Hidden King is in the unit}.

\subsection*{Winning the Secondary Objective}

\rewordedrule{A}t the end of the game, \rewordedrule{if} your \rewordedrule{Hidden K}ing is alive and the opponent's \rewordedrule{has been removed as a casualty}, you win th\rewordedrule{is} Secondary Objective.
