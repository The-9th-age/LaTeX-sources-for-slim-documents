\subsection*{Winning the Secondary Objective}

Players are awarded Victory Counters \rewordedrule{during the battle when the following events occur}:

\begin{itemize}
\item \rewordedrule{Each time a}n enemy Scoring Unit is \rewordedrule{removed as a casualty -- 2 counters}
\item \rewordedrule{Each time a}n enemy Character \rewordedrule{is killed} in \rewordedrule{a} Duel \rewordedrule{-- 2 counters} 
\item The first time a \rewordedrule{friendly non-Fleeing} Scoring Unit \rewordedrule{is inside} the enemy Deployment Zone \rewordedrule{-- 2 counters}
\item The first time a  friendly unit \rewordedrule{without Unbreakable or Unstable} passes a Break Test \rewordedrule{-- 1 counter}
\item \rewordedrule{Each time an enemy unit performs} a Flee Charge Reaction\rewordedrule{ -- 1 counter}
\item \rewordedrule{Each time a friendly unit} successfully \rewordedrule{C}harge\rewordedrule{s} an enemy unit in the Flank or Rear \rewordedrule{-- 1 counter}
\end{itemize}

At the end of the game, the player with the most Victory Counters wins th\rewordedrule{is} Secondary Objective.
