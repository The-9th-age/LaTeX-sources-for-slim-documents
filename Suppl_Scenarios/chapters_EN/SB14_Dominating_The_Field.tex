\subsection*{Deployment}

The \rewordedrule{board} is divided into quarters by \rewordedrule{two} straight lines through the centre of the board, parallel to the \rewordedrule{Board} \rewordedrule{E}dges. Deployment Zones are \rewordedrule{two opposite} corners\rewordedrule{,} and more than \rewordedrule{\distance{10}} away from the centre of the \rewordedrule{board}.

\subsection*{Winning the Secondary Objective}

At the end of the game, \rewordedrule{players are awarded} Victory Counters for each zone \rewordedrule{in which} they have at least one Scoring Unit:

\begin{itemize}
\item The opponent's Deployment Zone -- 3 counters
\item The zones adjacent to the Deployment Zones -- 2 counters \rewordedrule{each}
\item Your own Deployment Zone -- 1 counter
\item The \rewordedrule{centre} zone -- 0 counters
\end{itemize}

A unit is considered to be \rewordedrule{in a} zone \rewordedrule{if} its \rewordedrule{C}entre \rewordedrule{is} in\rewordedrule{side} \rewordedrule{it}.

The player with the most Victory Counters wins th\rewordedrule{is} Secondary Objective.
